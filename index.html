Designed Out

Discrimination in product language

About this survey

This survey collects examples of everyday physical products — objects — that communicate exclusion or discrimination through their formal aesthetic qualities: colour, material, shape, and form.

You will be asked to upload a photo or image of a product, share how it makes you feel, and optionally reflect on where you stand.

This survey is part of a PhD research project. Responses will be analysed and synthesised, and findings may be used for academic publication. Participation is entirely voluntary. The survey is completely anonymous — a random ID is assigned to each response and cannot be traced back to you.
